# How to Use the Paystub Calculator Web App

## Quick Start

1. **Open the Calculator**
   - Double-click `paystub_calculator.html` to open it in your default browser
   - Or right-click the file → "Open with" → Choose Chrome

2. **Fill in the Form**
   - Enter your gross pay per pay period
   - Select your state from the dropdown
   - Choose your employee status (US Citizen, Green Card, H1B, or OPT)
   - Select your filing status
   - Choose pay period frequency
   - Enter allowances and any additional withholding
   - (Optional) Enter year-to-date information

3. **Calculate**
   - Click the "Calculate Paystub" button
   - Results will appear on the right side automatically

## Features

✅ **All 50 States** - Complete tax calculations for every US state + DC
✅ **Employee Status Support** - OPT, H1B, Green Card, US Citizen
✅ **FICA Exemptions** - OPT employees can mark "First 2 Years" for FICA exemption
✅ **Real-time Calculation** - Results update automatically
✅ **Year-to-Date Tracking** - Track cumulative gross and net pay
✅ **Beautiful UI** - Modern, professional interface
✅ **No Internet Required** - Works completely offline

## Tips

- The calculator uses 2024 tax rates and brackets
- OPT employees should check "OPT First 2 Years" if they're in their first 2 years (FICA exempt)
- States with no income tax: AK, FL, NV, NH, SD, TN, TX, WA, WY
- The calculator automatically calculates based on IRS tax tables
